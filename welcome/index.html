Live on every phone

Share a round with one tap — everyone scores their own ball and every phone stays in sync, even when someone's in a signal dead zone until the cart crests the hill.

Handicaps handled

Real course ratings and slopes. Net, gross, or both at once — strokes land exactly where the card says they should, and solo net plays your full handicap.

GPS hole maps

Satellite view with live front · middle · back distances, plus a tap-anywhere rangefinder on mapped courses.

Playing Wolf but curious how the round scores as Stableford or Vegas? Tabs replay the same scores through any game, instantly.

Settled, not argued

Points become payouts automatically — who pays whom, to the quarter. The 19th-hole math is already done.

A personal round clock keeps pace honest, and every finished round is saved — reopen any past round to review or fix a score.
