Scramble

One ball per team

The basics

Everyone tees off, the team picks the best shot, and everyone plays their next shot from that spot. One score per team per hole. Lowest team total wins.

It's the format of charity events and member-guests everywhere — fast, forgiving, and social.

Handicaps

2 v 2: Battle Tees applies the USGA two-player scramble allowance — 35% of the low course handicap plus 15% of the high — as net strokes for the higher team.

One team (all four): tournament mode. The whole group plays one ball and the app keeps the official card with the 25/20/15/10% four-player allowance.

In Battle Tees

In one-team mode there's nothing to settle — hand the card to the committee. In 2 v 2, settlement pays by strokes of difference.
