Hammer

Battle modifier — double or fold

Not a game — a weapon

The Hammer is a battle modifier: switch it on in Best Ball or Sixes and every hole becomes a standoff.

Any time during a hole — even mid-swing drama — either team can throw the hammer. The hole instantly goes double-or-fold.

Accept or fold

Accept, and the double is live — and the hammer changes hands: only the accepting team can throw the next one. Re-hammers stack: 2×, 4×, 8×…

Fold, and you concede the hole at the old stake. Cheap insurance or slow bleed — your call.

Every hole starts fresh at 1× with the hammer in the air. Throw it when your opponent is in the trees.
