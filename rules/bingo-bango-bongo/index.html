Bingo Bango Bongo

Three taps a hole — the great equalizer

The basics

Three points on every hole: 🟢 Bingo — first ball on the green. 📍 Bango — once every ball is on, closest to the pin. ⛳ Bongo — first ball in the cup.

Most points at the end wins. No handicaps needed — the short hitter playing first has the inside track on Bingo, and lag putts win Bongos.

The one rule that matters

Play strictly in turn — farthest from the hole plays first. That etiquette IS the game; break it and the point is forfeit.

It's the great equalizer: a 30-handicap can beat a scratch player all day without a single stroke given.

In Battle Tees

Three taps per hole on the score screen, synced across every phone in the group. Scores still go in normally, so stroke play and the what-if tabs ride along.
