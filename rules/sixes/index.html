Sixes

Partners rotate every six holes — everyone teams with everyone

The basics

Sixes — also called Round Robin or Hollywood — is three matches in one round: partners swap every six holes (every three on a nine), so by the 18th everyone has teamed with everyone.

Each hole, better ball of each team wins a point for both partners. Tie on best ball? Both balls count. Still tied — push.

Why it works

Nobody is stuck with a cold partner all day, and the pairings mean your points travel with you — settlement is pairwise across all three partnerships, like Wolf.

Pick the starting partners in setup; Battle Tees handles both rotations automatically and shows the current teams on the hole screen.

Battle modifier

Sixes is one of the two games that can carry the 🔨 Hammer — double-or-fold on any hole, with the stake compounding 2× → 4× → 8×.
